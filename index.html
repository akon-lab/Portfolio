SWOT Script:

S-Strengths

  • The Test Center is doing a great job as one of the information sites of the Ministry of Education of Kazakhstan.
  • It has no competitors in our country.
  • Being one of the state websites, its resources are a certain database of the Ministry of Education related to state exams and tests.
  • many users of the site note its quick access and user-friendly interface

W-Weaknesses

  • One of the disadvantages of the site is its ordinary design. Although the main page of the site looks very attractive, but only it can boast of this kind, the rest of the pages look the same and many users it discourages the desire to familiarize themselves with the provided page.
  • The site may also hang in the period of passing the UNT, when 300 thousand users want to know the results, thus overloading the site traffic.
  • Though the Test Center has no direct competitors in Kazakhstan, but taking into account foreign sites with the same direction as ETS "Educational Testing Service“ https://www.ets.org/ (USA) or CITO https://www.cito.com/ (Nederland), it is already inferior to them according to some sources.
  • More precisely in such categories as Trust/Spam/Download speed.

O-Opportunities

  • Links to the recent pandemic have shown a trend towards distance learning, and many government tests could also go online if the site had sufficient resources to conduct such examinations with minimal cheating,
  • Even if the Test Center could provide this kind of test, there would still be a problem with Internet access, in many parts of the country.
  • In 2020, SNT will be conducted as before, but thanks to pandemics, the Ministry of Education already knows what they need to work on.

T-Threats

  • If the state exams are postponed online in the future, the database itself may be hacked during the exam and thus data leaks may occur. This is due to the fact that data protection becomes much weaker during the online regime. Therefore, the implementation of the online exam is assessed as an idea with a higher threat level.

Video Tutorial [Script]

[ HOOK]

Are you a novice web developer and you don't have a foundation to host your project? You don’t know how to get free hosting? Well, you're in the right place, my friend. In this video, I'm going to show you step by step how to host website on free GitHub hosting? Stay tuned.

[INTRO]

Hi, I'm Akniet, and on this channel, I share ICT tutorials for beginner programmers. This video will be useful for novice programmers in the field of web development. When you are newbie in ICT, and you need show your work, the name of the game is to host projects that make your portfolio looks professional and decent. You have two options. First option, pay for hosting and domain to place your work. Second option, use GitHub free hosting. I'm going to walk you through second one, after all GitHub provides excellent conditions for creating a portfolio of projects for a novice.

[ BODY]

  • First you need to register in GitHub.

If you don't have an account yet.

  • You just need to fill this form and press “Sign up for GitHub” button.
  • Now we need to create a repository.

Bonus: the repository is a special cell of your GitHub, where you can place your project (made in any language). Its superiority, because it also stores your code.

  • I sign in to my account, know we need to create new repository
  • On the right-top side you can see that “+” button, press it.
  • Then choose “New repository” and click
  • Now go to Settings
  • We are in Repository create constructor:
  • For the first we name our repository.
  • We should write some description.
  • Choose option of repository visibility,

Choose public or make it hidden, it is on your choice.

  • You can also make Readme. file, but I will do it next time, it is not important
  • After all, press that green button “Create”
  • we got our repository, you may be shock seeing that, but they are just hints to insert files.
  • Now we need download GitHub Desktop to insert project files from our computer

https://desktop.github.com/

  • After loading you can choose the way how GitHub Desktop will connect with your git-account
  • Then filling a form and press “Sign in” button
  • Find your repository
  • Click on “Clone (repository name)” button
  • In come up page Clone, press “Clone” [It mean that you agree to give access to repository]
  • Now you need to search file witch have the same name as your repository.
  • Replace your project file into repository file
  • Open your GitHub Desktop again
  • You may find that some files will come up
  • Now write a summary right here, description not important.
  • Press the button “Commit to master”.
  • And when “No local changes page” will come up.
  • Click on “Publish branch” button.
  • Go to GitHub page and look in your repository, there are fills which we have inserted
  • Now we need make hosting for our repository
  • On the top-right side of page, find “Settings” and click
  • And if you scroll down, you'll see “GitHub Pages” section.
  • In that section we have block “Source”, in which we need press “None” button.
  • Choose master branch source.
  • Now we published our site, and need to wait for a while to allow the software to read your files.
  • Hint: once the box is green, it notifies you that a link is ready.
  • Press that link and go to website
  • Now you can copy that link and share it

 

[ summery]

So you can start leveraging the power of GitHub to make more project with ease.
